ABOUT OUR BANK

BANK OF SPARK is India's largest private sector bank with total consolidated assets of Rs. 9,860.43 billion (US$ 152.0 billion) at March 15, 2015 and profit after tax of Rs. 98.01 billion (US$ 1.5 billion) for the year ended March 15, 2015. BANK OF SPARK Bank currently has a network of 3000+ Branches and 14000+ ATM's across India.

BANK OF SPARK was originally promoted in 1990 by BANK OF SPARK Limited, an Indian financial institution, and was its wholly-owned subsidiary.
